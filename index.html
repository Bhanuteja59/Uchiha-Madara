Madara Uchiha is a main antagonist manga and anime character in the Naruto series created by Masashi Kishimoto. He appears for the first time in "Part II" of the manga and the Shippuden anime adaptation, as a major antagonist. He is known for his power, skill, and being a founding member of Konoha alongside Hashirama Senju.
